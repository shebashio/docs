This privacy policy describes how the DEDZED platform collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services, including the Kasm Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI), ephemeral Kubernetes clusters, and associated tools.

## Information we collect

The DEDZED platform collects the following categories of information:

- **Account information** — your name, email address, and organizational affiliation as provided during registration through Ping Identity
- **Usage data** — cluster provisioning activity, session timestamps, and service access logs
- **Technical data** — browser type, connection metadata, and platform interaction logs necessary for security monitoring and support

## How we use your information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain platform access and services
- Monitor platform health, security, and performance
- Investigate and resolve technical issues
- Enforce the platform [terms and conditions](/support/terms-and-conditions)
- Communicate service updates and maintenance windows

## Data retention

- **Ephemeral environment data** is automatically destroyed when clusters expire. The platform does not retain workspace contents beyond the cluster lifecycle.
- **Account and access logs** are retained for the duration of your platform access and for a reasonable period afterward for security and audit purposes.

## Data protection

We protect your information through:

- Zero-trust network architecture with authenticated access at every layer
- Encryption in transit and at rest for all platform services
- Role-based access controls limiting data visibility to authorized personnel

## Third-party services

The DEDZED platform integrates with third-party services including Ping Identity for authentication and cloud infrastructure providers for cluster hosting. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

## Changes to this policy

The DEDZED team may update this privacy policy at any time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us via [Contact and support](/support/contact).
